在tieto时的英文自我介绍

  • 心路历程

Hey，all. My name is Andy. It is nearly one year since I joined in Tieto. I act as the software engineer. Being a member of Tieto, I tried to adapt myself to the new circumstance and the new culture. As we all known that, Tieto is a Finnish company which has five branches in the globe. Tieto Chengdu established in the last year. There is a saying that “New chance brings up new challenges”. So in my daily work I take more initiative and sometimes put a lot on the plate. In thus way,I have got more experience and enlarged my knowledge. That’s why I can take more responsibility in my future work.

In my spare time, I love reading, because one will easily lag behind unless he keeps on learning. In addition, I love to play PC games, such as war craft. Also having hotpot and drinks together with friends or colleagues are my favorites.
